Brand


Passavinti is a historic Florentine jewelry detected by the Reali srl in 2011. Founded in 1970 by the skillful goldsmith Roman Passavinti, the brand is now the result of the restyling of three young entrepreneurs who are looking for a constant quality work in line with the innovative spirit that has always characterized the master Passavinti.

Passavinti Silver


All jewelry Passavinti are the result of careful design and careful research in precision manufacturing. The decision to treat the silver in the manner of the ancient tradition of Italian gold jewelry mean that Passavinti become a creative act in which flows a centuries-old wisdom.

Passavinti Bronze


All creations Passavinti Bronze are strictly made in Italy and are the result of exclusive attention to design, shapes and quality materials. An innovative and dynamic type of clothing suitable for both eveningwear to work.
